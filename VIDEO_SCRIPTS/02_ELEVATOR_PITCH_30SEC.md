## BODY LANGUAGE & DELIVERY

**Opening (0:00-0:07):**
- Direct eye contact
- Slight lean forward
- Hand gesture: "3 to 5 years" (hold up fingers)

**Middle (0:07-0:20):**
- Confident posture
- Hand gesture: "One platform" (bring hands together)
- Slight nod on "production-ready"

**Close (0:20-0:30):**
- Smile
- Hand business card smoothly
- Maintain eye contact on "Here's my card"

## COMMON OBJECTIONS & RESPONSES

**OBJECTION:** *"Quantum computers aren't here yet."*
**RESPONSE:** *"They will be by 2030—and hackers are harvesting encrypted data TODAY to decrypt later. We need to be quantum-safe NOW."

**OBJECTION:** *"We use CrowdStrike/Palo Alto already."*
**RESPONSE:** *"They're scrambling to add quantum features. AMCIS was built quantum-native. We can integrate with them—or replace 8 tools with one platform. Want to see how?"

**OBJECTION:** *"This sounds expensive."*
**RESPONSE:** *"We save customers 40% over buying 8-12 separate tools. Plus, we automate compliance that costs millions in consulting fees. What's expensive is doing nothing."

**OBJECTION:** *"Send me an email."*
**RESPONSE:** *"I will—but give me 15 minutes next Tuesday. I'll show you our quantum cryptography engine in action. Deal?"
