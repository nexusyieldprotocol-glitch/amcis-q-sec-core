## 1. INTRODUCTION

Global Compliance Agency ("AMCIS," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AMCIS Q-SEC CORE software and services.

**CRITICAL NOTE:** AMCIS is primarily a self-hosted cybersecurity platform. In the standard self-hosted deployment, we do NOT have access to your security data, customer information, or system configurations. This Privacy Policy primarily applies to:
- Our website (amcis-security.com)
- Cloud-hosted services (if applicable)
- Customer support interactions
- Marketing communications

## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 2.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Name, email address, company name
- Billing information (processed by third-party payment processors)
- Support ticket information

**Communications:**
- Email correspondence
- Support chat transcripts
- Feedback and survey responses

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data (Website):**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Referral source
- Pages visited
- Time and date of visits

**Telemetry (Optional):**
With your explicit consent, AMCIS software may collect:
- Feature usage statistics
- Performance metrics
- Error reports (anonymized)

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect (Self-Hosted)

In standard self-hosted deployments, we do NOT collect or have access to:
- Your security event data
- Threat intelligence from your environment
- User credentials or passwords
- Encrypted data or keys
- Network traffic data
- Compliance assessment results

- Email service providers
- Analytics providers (Google Analytics)

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by:
- Court order or subpoena
- Law enforcement request
- Government regulation
- To protect our rights or safety

### 4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred.

We never sell, rent, or share:

## 5. DATA SECURITY

### 5.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits
- Employee training

### 5.2 Data Retention

We retain your information for:
- **Active Accounts:** Duration of subscription plus 30 days
- **Terminated Accounts:** 90 days (for legal compliance), then deleted
- **Financial Records:** 7 years (as required by law)
- **Anonymized Analytics:** Indefinitely
